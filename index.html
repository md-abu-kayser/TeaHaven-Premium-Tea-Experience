Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our teas, shipping, events, and more.

What makes your teas premium?

Our teas are hand-picked from sustainable farms, processed artisanally, and certified organic for superior quality and flavor.

How do I brew the perfect cup?

Follow the instructions on each package: water temperature, steeping time, and amount vary by type. We recommend using filtered water.

Are your teas organic?

Yes, 100% certified organic, free from pesticides, and sourced ethically.

What is your shipping policy?

Free shipping on orders over $50, worldwide delivery within 5-10 days, with tracking provided.

How can I join an event?

Book via the event page; spots are limited. Virtual options available for select workshops.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee if unsatisfied. Contact support for details.

Are your products vegan?

All teas are plant-based and vegan-friendly, with no animal products.

How to store tea?

Keep in airtight containers away from light, heat, and moisture for up to 2 years.

Can I customize a gift set?

Yes, contact us for personalized gift options with selected teas and accessories.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and more secure and encrypted.
